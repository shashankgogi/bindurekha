Bindurekha means a line of dots, a verse…

Founded in 2009 by the husband wife duo of Leena and Manish Gavande, Bindurekha is a venture by chance.  

About Leena Gavande :

Leena Gavande, Founder Designer of Bindurekha has worked in the HR department of IT companies like Sun Microsystems, Geometric, Bladelogic, BMC software and also has experience in being associated with start ups. The 2008 slow down in IT industry gave her a chance to rekindle her passion for jewellery designing and making.  

 She explains the transition from IT industry to fashion has been a culmination of an opportunity to pursue my passion of making  unique adornments into a very successful business venture.  

“I had managed to pursue my passion of designing one of kind jewellery simultaneously with a career in the software industry. I was never a gold jewellery buff and always wanted something unique and different suiting my tastes. I did not like the mass production jewellery in the showrooms and hence started designing my own. The focus was on creating something which was one of a kind or at least not readily available in the market.  

I have a huge circle of friends and many liked my style of jewellery and mix and match look and wanted me to design adornments for them and their friends. As requests and orders began to pile up the need for an organised business enterprise that will provide quality products and efficient service became apparent. The venture was named Bindurekha which means a line of dots / a verse and the first store was started in 2009 at INOX multiplex mall, Pune. Now we have scaled up from the initial days of beading and mix and match and now Bindurekha also specialises in metal smithing brass, copper and german silver into unique adornments. The products are acid etched / print fused with metal. The entire process is handmade and each product takes around 3-7 days.  

About Manish Gavande:

Manish is from commerce background and is an inter from the ICWAI. He has experience in project consultancy and taxation. He co-founded MI India an HR BPO and managed it successfully from 2003 to 2009. In 2009 he joined Bindurekha and started recruiting a small team to operate the concern in a professional manner, currently he looks after the operations and marketing of Bindurekha. He is the silent force in making Bindurekha a well recognised label in the fashion jewellery circle with a loyal customer base spread across the globe.
