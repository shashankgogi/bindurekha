2. How do I place an order?

  • Go to product detail you want to purchase & click on add to cart or buy now. Further go to my cart section from top right corner cart icon & proceed with with checkout process by adding delivery address & doing online payment through multiple payment gateway options.

3. How do I pay for the orders?

  • Whatever your mode of payment be rest assured our payment gateway partners use secure encryption technology to keep your transactions and information safe and secure. You can pay through net banking, debit and credit cards, wallets, UPI payment options.

4. Do I need an account at Bindurekha.com to place an order?

  • You do not need an account to place an order and can check out as a guest, but having an account will make your experience more smoother in future interactions.

5. What is the policy for exhchange, returns and refund?

  • Bindurekha has a strict no return and refund policy. Exchange of products is allowed only in case of a manufacturing defect if any in the product and the same has to be brought to our notice within 8 hours of receiving the product.

6. Is shipping free all over India and the world?

  • Shipping is free pan India, for international orders shipping is free for orders above Rs 7500 INR.

7. When do the customers get tracking details of their orders?

  • Bindurekha updates the tracking details within 12 hours of shipping the product. The customer can track his shipment online via the tracking number.
