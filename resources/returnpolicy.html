Cancellation Policy

We will be able to accept cancellation requests any time before we ship your order. Unfortunately we cannot cancel orders once the shipment is out of our warehouse for delivery. A cancelled order will result in a credit to the users credit card/account. Refunds will be processed in a form of credit to their Credit Card / Net Banking / Debit Card Account within standard operating lead times.

Return Policy

We understand returns are inevitable while shopping online..! So in the event if the product is damaged in transit or has a manufacturing defect, please write into help@bindurekha.com or call our customer service number +91 9860076370 and we will arrange for pickup if the order is from within India. However please do intimate us within 3 days of receiving the product for a hassle free return process. Transportation cost to return the product will be borne by us. Once we receive the product we will process your return and credit your account. For international order returns please send us back the parcel and we will refund you the shipping charges and process your refund.

To return a product

  1. Send an email to help@bindurekha.com or call our helpline number +91 9860076370 with your order number.
  2. Following your correspondence, you will receive a mailing address where you will need to ship the product along with the return ID that will be mentioned in the mail.
  3. Please put the product in its original packaging and seal it well and wait for our courier guy to pick the product.
  4. In case we can't arrange for a pick up, you can ship it back to us. We will reimburse the shipping charges against the original receipt.
  5. Send an email to help@bindurekha.com with the tracking number
  6. Once the product has reached us and it is inspected your money will be credited to the account from which payment was made.

Exceptions to return policy

There are certain scenarios where it is difficult for us to support returns.

  1. Return request is made outside the specified time frame (3 Days from receipt of product, including Sundays and public holidays).
  2. Anything missing from the package you've received including price tags, labels, original packing, freebies and accessories.
  3. Product is damaged due to misuse or Incidental damage.
  4. Products with tampered or missing serial numbers.
  5. Made-to-order products.

All returns which are not valid will not be accepted and will be sent back. We will keep you updated on the status of your return at each step, When we validate your return, we send you a return authorization mail. At the closure of your refund, we send you a closure mail. Given the nature of our products, we reserve the sole discretion to provide the resolution to any situation as we deem fit. Each return or exchange request is handled on a case by case basis.

Refunds will be processed through the same online mode in 8-10 working days

Orders outside India

For Orders placed from outside India, each return or exchange request will be attended on case to case basis. In case of any issue the customer is requested to contact us on help@bindurekha.com and we will offer the most appropriate solution.
